Privacy Policy

NSAT Application Effective: June 1, 2026 Version 1.0 India Only
Overview

What this document covers

This Privacy Policy describes how Noida International University ("NIU", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal data of candidates ("you") who use the NSAT (NIU Student Aptitude Test) mobile and web application.

NSAT is used exclusively to conduct the NIU entrance aptitude test for admission candidates. By using the application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

ℹ This application is designed for India-based admission candidates only and is governed by applicable Indian law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.
Section 01

Data we collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to verify your identity, confirm your eligibility, and conduct the test.

Data Point How it is obtained Purpose
NIU ID (Application No.) Entered by you Identity verification & fee gate check
Name Fetched from NIU / NoPaperForms API — not entered by you Display on screen and result record
Email address Fetched from NIU / NoPaperForms API Sending the one-time verification code
Mobile number Fetched from NIU / NoPaperForms API Identity confirmation (not stored)
Course of interest Fetched from NIU / NoPaperForms API Loading the correct question paper
Test answers Submitted by you during the test Server-side scoring (answers are never stored — only the score)
Test result (score) Computed server-side Result display and admission processing
Attempt timestamp Recorded automatically One-attempt enforcement and audit
FCM device token Generated automatically by the device Delivering push notifications about test schedule and updates
Payment status Fetched from NIU / NoPaperForms API Verifying that your application fee has been received before granting test access
Short-answer responses Submitted by you during the test Stored for manual review by the NIU Admissions team
✓ We do not collect passwords, payment details, device location, biometrics, or any data not listed above. Name, email, mobile, and course are fetched directly from our systems — you do not enter them.
Section 02

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Verifying that your NIU ID corresponds to a registered, fee-paid applicant
  • Sending a one-time password (OTP) to your registered email to confirm your identity before the test
  • Loading the correct test paper for your chosen course
  • Scoring your test responses server-side and recording your result
  • Preventing duplicate attempts (one attempt per NIU ID)
  • Sending push notifications about test schedules, exam-day updates, and results (via Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • Providing the NIU Admissions team with result data for admission processing

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the above.

Section 02a

Data deletion

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting the NIU Admissions Department at admissions@niu.edu.in with the subject line "NSAT Data Deletion Request" and your NIU ID.

Upon receiving a valid request, we will delete or anonymise your data within 30 days, except where retention is required for legitimate institutional record-keeping (such as finalised admission decisions). We will confirm the deletion in writing.

ℹ Deleting your data while the admission cycle is active may affect your eligibility. You will be informed of any consequences before deletion proceeds.
Section 03

Storage & retention

All data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, hosted in the asia-south1 (Mumbai) region, ensuring your data stays within India.

Data Retention period
OTP records 10 minutes from generation, then automatically deleted
Attempt lock record Duration of the admission cycle
Test results (score & breakdown) 6 months in the app database, then transferred to NIU Admissions Department records
Student list (NPF sync) Refreshed every 30 minutes; reflects current NoPaperForms data
FCM device tokens Retained while the application is installed; revoked on uninstall
Short-answer responses Retained with results for admissions review; same 6-month policy as test results
ℹ After 6 months, result data is retained by the NIU Admissions Department under institutional records policies. The NSAT application database no longer holds a copy.
Section 04

Third-party services

The NSAT application uses the following third-party infrastructure. No data is sold to or shared with any third party for commercial purposes.

  • Google Firebase / Firestore — database and serverless functions. Data processed under Google's data processing terms.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — push notification delivery. FCM processes a device token (not personal data) to deliver notifications. No message content is stored by FCM after delivery.
  • NoPaperForms (Meritto) — our admissions CRM. Applicant details are fetched from this system; NIU controls the data stored there.
  • SMTP email provider (Gmail) — used only to deliver the one-time OTP email. No email content is retained by the provider beyond delivery.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics trackers, or social media platforms.

Section 05

Your rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data, subject to legitimate institutional record-keeping requirements
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (note: this will prevent you from using the NSAT application)
  • Raise a grievance with the Data Protection Board of India if your concerns are not resolved

To exercise any of these rights, contact the NIU Admissions Department at the address below.

Section 06

Security measures

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • Test answers are scored server-side; the answer key is never sent to or stored on your device
  • OTPs are hashed before storage and expire after 10 minutes with a 5-attempt limit
  • Firestore security rules restrict read and write access to authorised functions and authenticated admins
  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS
  • Admin access is protected by email and password authentication
Section 07

Children & minors

NSAT is intended for admission candidates who are typically 17 years of age or older. The application is not directed at children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

For candidates between the ages of 13 and 18, use of the application is presumed to be with the knowledge and consent of a parent or guardian, as the admission process is typically initiated by a family.

If you believe a child under 13 has submitted data through this application, please contact us immediately at admissions@niu.edu.in so we can promptly delete it.

Section 08

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the application and/or the NIU admissions portal. Continued use of the application after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. The effective date at the top of this document indicates the version currently in force.
