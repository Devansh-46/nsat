NSAT Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026 · Last updated: May 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Noida International University ("NIU", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information through the NSAT (NIU Student Aptitude Test) mobile application and web platform ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use the App:

Data Purpose Source
Full name Identify the test-taker and display on results NoPaperForms (NPF) application
Email address Send one-time password (OTP) for login verification NPF application
Phone number Stored in NPF records; not directly used by the App NPF application
NIU Application Number Unique identifier for login and test-attempt tracking NPF application
Test responses & scores Evaluate aptitude test performance and generate results Collected in-app during the test
FCM device token Deliver push notifications about exam updates Collected automatically from device
Payment status Verify fee payment before granting test access NPF application

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for administering the NIU entrance examination. Specifically, we use it to verify your identity and fee payment status, send OTP codes for secure login, deliver your aptitude test, calculate and display your test results, and send push notifications related to the examination.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. The App contains no advertisements.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, hosted in the asia-south1 (Mumbai) region. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), hashed OTP codes (SHA-256) with automatic expiry, server-side test scoring to prevent tampering, and Firebase security rules that restrict data access by role.

Student data (name, email, course) is fetched live from the NoPaperForms CRM API during your session and is not permanently stored in the App's database. Only your application number, payment status, and test results are persisted.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

Service Provider Purpose
Firebase (Firestore, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging, Crashlytics, Analytics) Google LLC Database, serverless backend, push notifications, crash reporting, usage analytics
NoPaperForms CRM API NoPaperForms Pvt. Ltd. Student application data and fee status verification
SMTP (Gmail) Google LLC Sending OTP verification emails

No data is shared with advertising networks, data brokers, or any other third parties beyond those listed above.

5. Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics to understand general usage patterns (such as screen views and session duration) and Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports for improving app stability. These services may collect device identifiers, app usage data, and crash logs. This data is processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy and is used solely for improving the App.

6. Push Notifications

The App uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications about examination schedules, updates, and results. Your device token is collected for this purpose. You may disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

7. Data Retention

Test results and attempt records are retained for the duration of the admission cycle (academic year 2026–27). OTP records are automatically deleted after 10 minutes. Student sync data is refreshed every 30 minutes and reflects the current state of the NPF CRM. After the admission cycle concludes, test data may be archived or deleted in accordance with NIU's records policy.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to the personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data (subject to academic record-keeping requirements), and opt out of push notifications via your device settings. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for prospective university students. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
